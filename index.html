I am a specialist Prosthodontist and Implantologist currently consulting in Mumbai, with a passion for transforming lives through confident, beautiful smiles. My practice is built around the most complex and rewarding cases in dentistry.

I completed my Masters in Prosthodontics in 2020 from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bengaluru, and hold an international Prosthodontic license from Saudi Arabia, with hands-on clinical experience at Al-Flaiw Medical Polyclinic, Saudi Arabia.
